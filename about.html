About OE Star

Delving into the history, values, and vision of the Operational Excellence Systems Technology Analysis & Research group at the University of Florence.

Our Story

The OE Star research group was founded with the ambition to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical application in the field of Operational Excellence. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated researchers at the University of Florence, driven by a shared passion for optimizing complex systems and developing innovative technologies. Over the years, we have grown into a multidisciplinary team, consistently contributing to the academic community and collaborating with industry leaders to solve real-world challenges.
