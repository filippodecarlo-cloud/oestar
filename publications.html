Advancements in Predictive Maintenance for Smart Manufacturing Systems

A. Rossi, B. Bianchi, C. Verdi

Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing, Vol. 35, Issue 2, pp. 245-260, 2024.

View Abstract expand_more

This paper presents a novel framework for predictive maintenance in smart manufacturing environments. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and real-time sensor data, our model significantly improves the accuracy of failure prediction, thereby reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

Lean Six Sigma in Healthcare: A Case Study on Emergency Department Workflow Optimization

M. Conti, F. De Luca, G. Esposito

Proceedings of the International Conference on Industrial Engineering and Operations Management, pp. 1120-1135, 2023.

View Abstract expand_more

This study applies Lean Six Sigma principles to optimize patient flow and reduce waiting times in a hospital emergency department. The implemented changes resulted in a 30% reduction in average patient stay and a significant increase in patient satisfaction scores.
